What is HTML? Why use it!

HTML - Hyper Text Markup language
HTML vs HTML5
Head Tags: Meta tag information about the document
Body Tag: Contain all of your documents.
Element: Tag + Content
Example: <h1>Welcome</h1>

Heading Tag: H1...H6 

A tag:
To redirect the document, we use "a tag/anchor tag"
Example:
<a href="https://www.guvi.in/" target="_blank">Google</a>
attribute: it gives the additional information about the tag.
href: represent the link where it has to be redirected.
target: help us to open the documment on the new tab.
